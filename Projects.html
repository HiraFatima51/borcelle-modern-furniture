Our Eight Step Process

From enquiry to installation

01

Enquiry

Send drawings or references with quantities, finishes and timeline

02

Scope and Budget

We review scope, propose materials and confirm budgets and lead times

03

Concept and sampling

Finish boards, fabric and timber samples prepared for approval

04

Prototype

Pre-production sample built to test comfort, joinery and details

05

Manufacture

Batch production using contract-grade frames, foams and finishes

06

Quality and compliance

Checks for dimensions and finish; fire and durability certificates on request

07

Logistics

Export packing, documentation and global freight with tracking

08

Delivery and installation

On-site assembly and snagging for a seamless handover
