About Us

Founded in 2014, Borcelle is a Pakistan-based furniture and interior design company built on the belief that great design should be accessible, functional, and long-lasting. Established by Sana Sheikh, Nadia Farid, Hira Fatima with a love for craftsmanship, Borcelle brings together creativity and practicality to shape modern living spaces.

Borcelle specializes in custom-made furniture, interior design, and fit-out services for homes, offices, commercial outlets, and hospitality projects across Pakistan. Our Design Studio, led by Ayesha, focuses on human-centered design — creating spaces that reflect comfort, beauty, and the unique lifestyle of every client.

With a team of skilled designers, architects, craftsmen, and 3D visualizers, Borcelle delivers projects with precision, originality, and a strong commitment to quality.
